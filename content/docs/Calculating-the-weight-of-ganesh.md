### Mass-Energy Content of the Observable Universe

The observable universe is estimated to have a mass of around \(1.5 \times 10^{53}\) kg. This includes ordinary matter, dark matter, and dark energy.

### Assumption for Calculation

We assume Ganesh’s weight is equivalent to the mass-energy of the observable universe.

### Estimation Steps

1. **Mass of the Observable Universe**:
   \[
   M_{U} \approx 1.5 \times 10^{53} \text{ kg}
   \]

2. **Weight Calculation**:
   - Based on this assumption, Ganesh's weight would be:
   \[
   \text{Weight of Ganesh} \approx 1.5 \times 10^{53} \text{ kg}
   \]
